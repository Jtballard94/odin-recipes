Picanha

Brazils little secret

what a beautiful cut of meat

This cut of meat is one of those cuts that goes under the radar. Very popular in brazilian steak houses, but not seen much in the US. I enjoy cooking this cut many different ways, but for this post ill keep it as a roast. Look out for more content on how to cook it as steaks. As a roast think tritip, but add more flavor, tenderness and a better fat cap.

What you will need

  • Picanha
  • Smoker/Grill
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder, and onion powder
  • cast iron skillet

How to cook the Picanha

As I said before there are many ways too cook this cut. today we'll reverse sear it.

  1. Pat your roast dry and add your seasonings
  2. let it sit as your smoker or oven is getting up to temp
  3. once the ven is at temp heat a cast iron skillet and coat with butter
  4. Sear the fat cam first then the rest of the roast, this should just be a quick sear to brown the meat.
  5. If cooking in the oven you can leave it on the cast iron but i prefer a baking sheet with a cooling rack on it
  6. Once the roast reaches an internal temp of 135, pull it out and let it rest for at least 5 minutes
  7. Then slice and enjoy with your favorite sides, maybe some gravy or chimichurri
