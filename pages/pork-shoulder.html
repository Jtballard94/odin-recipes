Pork Shoulder

Pulled pork is perfect for almost any weeknight meal prep.

What you'll need

  • Smoker or oven
  • Olive oil, Salt, pepper, garlic, brown sugar
  • tin foil or peach paper

How to cook

  1. preheat smoker or oven too 250
  2. Lather up the butt with olive oil as a binder
  3. Add the seasoning
  4. Puth the shoulder on the smoker
  5. Wrap the butt at 160 degree
  6. Pull the pork off at 205
  7. Either let rest or shred with forks as its hot.
